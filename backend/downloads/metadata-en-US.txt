REVIEW NOTES
------------------------------------------------------------
• AquaPulse is a 100% free water-tracking app — no ads, no IAPs,
  no subscriptions, no paywalls.
• "Sign in with Apple" is fully implemented as required by App Store
  Guideline 4.8 (alongside Google Sign-In and Guest mode).

  (required by App Store Guideline 5.1.1(v)). It permanently removes
  the user record, chat history and family membership server-side.

  are all native in-app features — no external content.
