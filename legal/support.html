Frequently Asked Questions

Is AquaPulse really free?

Yes. No ads, no subscriptions, no in-app purchases. We may add optional premium features later, always clearly labeled.

Do I need an account to use it?

No. You can use AquaPulse fully as a guest. An account is only needed for Family Mode and to sync across devices.

How is my data protected?

Guests' data stays on-device. Signed-in users' data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest. See our Privacy Policy.

How do I delete my account?

Open the App → Settings → "Permanently delete my account". You'll be asked to type DELETE / SİL to confirm. Or email us.

What is the AI Coach? Is it safe?

AquaCoach uses OpenAI's gpt-4o to give educational hydration & breath tips personalized to your profile. It's NOT medical advice — consult a doctor for health decisions.

Notifications aren't working — what to do?

Go to iOS Settings → AquaPulse → Notifications → enable. Then in AquaPulse → Settings → Reminders → toggle ON and pick interval.

Does AquaPulse support my language?

AquaPulse is fully translated into 18 languages: Turkish, English, German, French, Spanish, Italian, Portuguese, Dutch, Russian, Arabic, Japanese, Korean, Chinese, Hindi, Indonesian, Vietnamese, Thai, Polish.

How do I create or join a Family group?

Sign in with Apple or Google first. Then open the Family card on Home → choose "Create family" (you get a 6-character code to share) or "Join family" (enter the code your member sent you).

Can I export my data?

Yes. Email us at info@ttbinternationalllc.com and we'll send your data in JSON within 7 days.

I have a bug or feature idea

Email us at info@ttbinternationalllc.com with details. We read every message and ship updates weekly.
