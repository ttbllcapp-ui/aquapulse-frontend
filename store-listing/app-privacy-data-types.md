# Apple App Privacy — Data Types (App Store Connect form)

Fill these exactly when completing "App Privacy" in App Store Connect.

## Contact Info
- **Email Address** — Linked to user, NOT used for tracking. Purpose: App Functionality, Account Management.
- **Name** — Linked to user, NOT used for tracking. Purpose: App Functionality (display in Family leaderboard).

## User Content
- **Other User Content** (AquaCoach chat messages, family invite codes, family progress) — Linked to user, NOT used for tracking. Purpose: App Functionality, Customer Support.

## Health & Fitness
- **Health** (water intake) — Linked to user (if signed in) or unlinked (guest). Purpose: App Functionality. NOT used for tracking, NOT for advertising.
- **Fitness** (streak days, daily goal completion) — Same as above.

## Identifiers
- **User ID** — generated by us (UUID). Linked to user, NOT used for tracking.

## NOT collected
- Location ❌
- Photos or Videos ❌
- Audio Data ❌
- Camera ❌
- Browsing History ❌
- Search History ❌
- Advertising Data ❌
- Diagnostics (no Firebase / no Sentry) ❌
- Purchases ❌
- Financial Info ❌
- Contacts ❌
- Sensitive Info ❌

## Third-party SDKs
- None. We use only first-party Apple/Google sign-in and our own backend.

## Tracking
- We do NOT track users across apps or websites.
- We do NOT share data with data brokers.

## Account deletion
- Yes, in-app at Settings → "Permanently delete my account" (App Store guideline 5.1.1(v) compliant).
